# **App Name**: Inventory Ace

## Core Features:

- Authentication: Secure user authentication with login, registration, forgot password, and change password functionalities.
- User and Role Management: Manage users, assign roles (Admin, Manager, Staff, Custom), and define permissions for each role, controlling access to different modules and functionalities.
- Dashboard: Provides a real-time overview of key business metrics, including total products, stock value, low stock alerts, sales data, and recent activities.
- Product Management: Enables adding, editing, and managing product information, including SKU, barcode, category, brand, pricing, stock levels, expiry dates, and images.
- Category Management: Organize products into categories and subcategories for easy browsing and reporting.
- Warehouse Management: Manage multiple warehouses or store locations, track stock levels in each location, and facilitate stock transfers between warehouses.
- Supplier Management: Maintain a database of suppliers, including contact information, payment terms, and purchase history.
- Purchase Management: Create and manage purchase orders, track incoming shipments, and record supplier invoices.
- Sales/POS System: A point-of-sale system for processing sales transactions, generating invoices, and managing customer payments. Supports barcode scanning and multiple payment methods.
- Customer Management: Maintain a database of customers, including contact information, purchase history, and credit limits.
- Reporting Module: Generate various reports, including stock report, sales report, purchase report, profit & loss, low stock report, expiry report, and tax report. (Paid Feature)
- Notifications and Alerts: Configure notifications and alerts for low stock levels, expiring products, and overdue payments. Supports email, WhatsApp, and SMS notifications. (Paid Feature)
- Settings: Configure business profile, invoice settings, tax settings, backup & restore options, and language/currency preferences.
- Subscription and Monetization: Manage subscription plans (Free, Pro, Enterprise) and billing information.
- Security and Logs: Implement security measures, including activity logs, data backups, role-based access control, and audit trails.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: White (#FFFFFF) to convey cleanliness and neutrality.
- Secondary color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for backgrounds to ensure readability and a modern look.
- Accent color: Red (#FF0000) for positive actions and alerts, such as successful transactions or stock replenishment.
- Headline font: 'Roboto' for a modern, readable, and professional appearance.
- Body font: 'Open Sans' for clear and accessible text throughout the application.
- Use simple, consistent icons from a library like Material Icons for a unified look.
- Employ a responsive, grid-based layout to ensure usability across different devices.
- Implement a clear separation of main navigation on the left and content on the right, similar to the reference image.
- Use data visualization widgets on the dashboard, presented in a clean and organized manner.
- Incorporate a 'Quick Add' button prominently for frequently used actions.
- Subtle animations for transitions and feedback to enhance user experience without being distracting.
